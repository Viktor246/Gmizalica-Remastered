This masterpiece of a web application was made by: Viktor Antunovic, Marin Frankic and Filip Kapetanovic,
as a project for the web application development course on the University of tehnical studies in Rijeka.


This web application is our spin on the widely popular, classic game of Snake.
Test your snake skills and compete with others and unlock different background and snake colors.


Disclaimer: you unlock background colors every 1500 points on your total score, and you unlock different snake colors every 150 on your highscore.
